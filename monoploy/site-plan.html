Overview

Purpose

I want to make a website to play monopoly online. This website will be a monopoly game in a simple and easy way to play. There are very few options to play monopoly online with most of them being bad knock-offs. You might have heard of monopoly plus that is available on most platforms, but have you every tried to play it? It is laggy with bad connection bugs that will frequently cause the game to crash. You could be a hour into the game and it just crashes the game. I want to create a simply website that will reliably host the game. I want to create this site for friends and family so we can have an easy way to play monoploy together.
